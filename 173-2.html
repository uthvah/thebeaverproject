Buster Jønsson

Director

Buster Jønsson represents the new generation of directors - the generation that took filmmaking into their own hands. Starting as an editor and sound designer, he honed his storytelling skills and as a director he has been able to add layers of emotion and striking visuals. Buster is still young, but thanks to his stunning work ethic he has quickly established himself as a creative force with a distinctive style. His work is never boring as he constantly seeks to balance the story and imagery, in engaging and exciting ways.
