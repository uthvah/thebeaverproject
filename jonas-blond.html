Jonas Blond

Director

Jonas Blond is a Director of Photography and Shooting Director based in Copenhagen. With extensive experience in various forms of media, including commercials, long-form fiction, documentaries and music videos, Jonas is passionate about pushing the boundaries of traditional storytelling. His portfolio includes multiple TV series and one feature film, showcasing his versatility and expertise. Known for his meticulous attention to detail and thorough preparation, Jonas approaches every project with a unique vision and an unwavering commitment to delivering visually stunning results. His passion for exploring the intersection between different genres allows him to bring fresh perspectives and captivating visuals to every project, making him a sought-after collaborator in the industry.
