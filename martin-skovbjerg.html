Martin Skovbjerg

Director

Martin is a real renaissance man. As an established DJ and a member of the musical collective AV AV AV he has built a remarkable musical career alongside his film work. He’s an extremely versatile director who manages to infuse all kinds of films with his own sensibility. Martin has directed two award winning features (Sticks and Stones, 2018 and Copenhagen Does Not Exist, 2023) and a wide variety of documentaries, music videos, commercials and TV series.
